Tacos
Tacos is a easy recipe which is made using cooked kidney beans and spices. The whole thing is filled inside crisp shell with cheese, onions, lettuce and tomatoes. You can buy taco shell in super market these days they are easily available. Or you can make it your own. Will share a separate recipe for taco shells. You can find it in amazon if you cannot find it in local shop.
Tacos Course: Main. Cuisine: Mexican. Servings: 10 tacos.
Ingredients
  1. Kidney Beans / Rajma - 2 cups
  2. Olive Oil - 3 tblsp
  3. Onion - 2 chopped finely
  4. Garlic - 6 cloves chopped finely
  5. Green Chillies - 2 chopped finely
  6. Tomato Puree - 1 cup
  7. Oregano - 2 tsp
  8. Cumin Powder - 2 tsp
  9. Salt to taste
  10. Pepper - 1 tsp
  11. Red Chilli Flakes - 1 tsp
    FOR ASSEMBLING
  1. Taco Shell - 12
  2. Cheese - 1 cup shredded
  3. Onion - 1 chopped finely
  4. Tomato - 1 chopped finely
  5. Lettuce - 2 cups shredded
  6. Coriander leaves as needed
Instructions
  1. Soak kidney beans overnight. Pressure cook for 4 to 5 whistle. Set aside,
  2. Heat oil and saute onions, garlic and chillies. Cook till golden.
  3. Add in beans along with cooking liquid, salt, all spices and mix well. Add in tomato puree and mix well.
  4. Mash it well using a masher.
  5. Simmer for 15 mins or so.
  6. Now bake taco shell in oven as per package directions.
  7. Fill with beans, cheese, onions, tomatoes, coriander and lettuce.
  8. Serve.
